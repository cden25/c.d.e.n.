About Network

A Dynamic Platform for Academic and Professional Brilliance

The Criminology Development and Excellence Network (CDEN) is a dynamic platform established to promote academic excellence, professional development, research collaboration, leadership, and community engagement within the field of criminology and related disciplines.

CDEN believes that sustainable national development depends on knowledgeable professionals who are committed to justice, security, ethical leadership, and evidence-based solutions to crime and social challenges.

By connecting students, educators, researchers, practitioners, and institutions, CDEN creates opportunities for learning, networking, innovation, and lifelong professional growth.
